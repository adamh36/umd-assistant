Add management skills to your program at the University of Michigan-Dearborn with a minor from the College of Business. It’s available to all UM-Dearborn students except those in the College of Business.The 21-credit minor requires a prerequisite in college algebra or pre-calculus. The minor itself consists of four required courses and three electives you choose from upper-level courses offered by the College of Business. The required courses cover financial accounting, information systems in management, marketing, and organizational behavior.
