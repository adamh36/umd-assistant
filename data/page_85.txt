The EIC provides opportunities for students and community members of all ages to explore and learn about nature first hand in the 120-acre Environmental Study Area. The EIC offers many interesting and educational outdoor experiences for various audiences and covering a range of environmental topics. For example, the Birding By Ear program introduces visually impaired students to birding and the outdoors. Many different programs are offered throughout the year that let you experience the different seasons. Follow the links below to learn more about what we have to offer.
