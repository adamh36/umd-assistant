Each winner is chosen through a thorough process that examined their academic achievements and honors, their leadership on campus and their contributions in the community. The students are recommended by their faculties and ultimately selected by each dean. Chancellor's and Deans' Medallion Awards Faculty Awards Each year the University of Michigan-Dearborn recognizes and honors those faculty members who have made a significant impact on their profession, scholarship and teaching across campus, in our community, and beyond. Faculty awards include the Distinguished Academic Citizenship Award, Collegiate Lectureships Award (two awards), Lecturer Excellence in Inclusive Teaching Award, Distinguished Research Team Award, Distinguished Research Award, and Distinguished Teaching Awards (two awards).
