About MI GEAR UPGEAR UP (Gaining Early Awareness and Readiness for Undergraduate Programs) is a national college access program designed to increase the number of students who enroll in and succeed in postsecondary education. The program supports students by providing the tools and resources needed to prepare for college.MI GEAR UP, funded by the U.S. Department of Education, focuses on increasing the number of low-income students who are prepared to enter postsecondary education. The program partners with Michigan’s 15 public universities and community organizations to provide early intervention services, academic support, and scholarship opportunities for participating students. The MI GEAR UP Program serves a cohort of students starting in seventh grade and continues to support them through their first year of college.
