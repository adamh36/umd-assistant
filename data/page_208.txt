The energy and sustainability certificate introduces you to some of the key issues and technologies involved in creating a brighter, greener world.You can apply the nine credits you earn for the certificate to an undergraduate mechanical engineering degree program at the University of Michigan-Dearborn. All classes are offered on campus, and some are occasionally offered online. Program Details What You'll Study You design your own certificate program, choosing three courses from eight options. They cover topics like wind turbines, thermal system design, solar energy systems, advanced vehicle energy systems, and internal combustion engines, among others. The courses emphasize critical thinking, problem solving, and innovative design, all while embedding a strong commitment to sustainability and renewable energy.
