You also can choose a few courses from other disciplines as listed in the UM-Dearborn Catalog. The AAST courses cover subjects like public history in Arab Detroit, Arabs and Muslims in media, Arab Americans since 1890, Arab American identity, and Middle Eastern diasporas, among others.
