Student-athletes at UM-Dearborn are proud to wear Michigan’s Maize and Blue. UM-Dearborn is a member of the National Association for Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA). Eight of our nine varsity teams compete in the Division II Wolverine-Hoosier Athletic Conference against our regional rivals like Concordia, Aquinas and Northwestern Ohio. Our men’s ice hockey team competes in the Division I Great Lakes Collegiate Hockey League of the American Collegiate Hockey Association. We also offer seven club sports and a variety of intramurals, from men’s and women’s bowling to co-ed broomball. Students can also stay active at the Wellness Center, which offers a range of exercise equipment, weights and fitness classes. Keep Up with the Wolverines Get a front-row seat at the Fieldhouse to watch the Wolverines take on our Division II rivals and cheer on the Big Ten Wolverines in Ann Arbor.
