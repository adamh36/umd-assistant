All classes are offered on campus, and some are occasionally offered online. Program Details What You’ll Study You design your own certificate program, choosing three courses from seven options. They cover topics like structural mechanics and design, mechanical vibrations, sound and noise controls, and biomechanics, among others. These courses will help you deepen your understanding of engineering principles, methods of computational analysis, and contemporary applications within various industries.
