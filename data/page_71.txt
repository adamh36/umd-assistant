Breadcrumb Home Events EIC 25th Anniversary: Stewardship Saturday EIC 25th Anniversary: Stewardship Saturday For: Everyone Saturday, May 16, 2026 2:30 pm - 4:30 pm Environmental Interpretive Center, Main Lobby, Outdoor in Environmental Study Area (map) Come out to the EIC this Saturday for a volunteer event from 1-4pm.City construction on Evergreen Road impacts travel to campus; please see the updated UM Dearborn Maps and Directions for suggested alternate routes.Each month will bring opportunities to remove various invasive plants in accordance with current best management practices, etc. Children must be at least 10 years of age to participate.All equipment for removing invasive plants will be provided by the Center, but if you can bring a sturdy pair of long-handled branch loppers, please do. Also, please bring your own drinking water and work gloves for use in the field.This activity involves moderate physical activity in off-trail natural habitat areas. Wear suitable work clothes that can be scraped and poked by twigs and/or the occasional thorny plant, or have contact with sticky bur seeds. A good pair of sturdy work shoes/boots is also a must.
