Our undergraduate certificate program introduces you to this rapidly evolving field, blending mechanical and electrical engineering to deliver a comprehensive understanding of innovative technological systems.You can apply the nine credits you earn for the certificate to an undergraduate mechanical engineering degree program at the University of Michigan-Dearborn. All classes are offered on campus, and some are occasionally offered online. Program Details What You’ll Study It’s up to you. To earn the certificate, you complete three courses from six options that cover mechanical vibrations, sound and noise controls, mechanical vibration and noise control, mechatronic systems, industrial robots, and linear systems control. The courses emphasize practice-based learning.
