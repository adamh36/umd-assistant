The leadership focuses on using best practices, data, and analysis in innovative ways to inform decisions and develop services and processes that ensure the best possible experience for students, from prospect through graduation. Whether collaborating with campus on enrollment strategies, or executing daily the critical work that goes into helping to guide students through the enrollment life cycle; the following teams carry out this important work. The Organizational Chart of the Division shows the overall structure of this unit. Strategy and Support One-Stop Student Services Undergraduate Enrollment Graduate Enrollment International Affairs Financial Aid & Scholarships Student AffairsStudent Affairs offers support and engagement opportunities for students, providing high-quality learning experiences beyond the classroom that enhance the sense of community on campus. Programs and activities range from civic engagement and leadership development to Greek life, campus events, student organizations, and programs and initiatives to support cultural and gender diversity as well as veterans. Counseling and psychological services and disability and accessibility services are also provided through Student Affairs.
