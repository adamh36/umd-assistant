The certificate program offers you the opportunity to deepen your understanding of engineering design in the mechanical engineering area.You can apply the nine credits you earn for the certificate to an undergraduate mechanical engineering degree program at the University of Michigan-Dearborn. All classes are offered on campus, and some are occasionally offered online. Program Details What You’ll Study You create your own certificate program, choosing three courses from among more than 20 options. They cover topics like structural mechanics and design, solar energy systems, sustainable energy and the environment, advanced vehicle energy systems, mechanical vibrations, sound and noise controls, advanced biomechanics, design of automotive chassis and body systems, and many others.
