Tableau Public For better data self-service, we provide public dashboards for enrollment and retention data Tableau Internal For a better data-self service to our internal audience, we provide a more comprehensive set of analytical tools. These dashboards have a more detailed level of filtering and a wider option of views. WebFOCUS WebFOCUS is an enterprise reporting tool that allows end users to run reports and receive operational data without having to request that information from our office each time they need it. Published Data (IPEDS, CDS, ETC.) Here you can find our official reported counts to surveys such as IPEDS and the Common Data Set. Surveys Here you can find survey response data to institutional surveys that our office administers on a rolling basis, such as the National Survey of Student Engagement.
