Program Details What You'll Study In this 30-credit program, you'll build a strong foundation in marketing analytics, digital marketing, and AI technologies. Core courses cover key topics such as marketing management, digital marketing strategies, and marketing research methods, alongside cutting-edge analytics techniques. You'll gain expertise in applying AI to enhance customer targeting and decision-making. Additionally, you'll have the opportunity to select electives that allow you to delve into specialized areas like product development, project management, or business analytics, tailoring your education to meet career goals. Transfer Credit and Course Exemptions Previous coursework deemed substantially similar to MKT 515, if completed with a grade of "B" or better, may qualify to exempt students from that course. Exempt courses must be replaced with other approved courses in the degree program.In addition, up to 9 transfer credits for previous equivalent graduate coursework can be applied to the degree if those credits have not been counted toward a degree.Exemptions and transfer credit are granted at the discretion of the program faculty. Admission Requirements This program welcomes students from all undergraduate majors and levels of work experience. We conduct a holistic review of each application, focusing on strong academic performance, quantitative skills, and a clear statement of purpose. Admission is selective.
